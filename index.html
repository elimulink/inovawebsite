About Us

InovaRoute is a technology-driven company focused on developing AI-powered indoor navigation and smart facility solutions. Our mission is to make malls, airports, hospitals, and other large complexes easier, safer, and smarter for visitors, tenants, and management.

Our Mission

To revolutionize how people move and interact within large facilities by delivering simple, secure, and intelligent navigation solutions.

Our Vision

To become Africa’s leading provider of indoor navigation and smart facility management solutions, making spaces more connected, accessible, and user-friendly.

Our Solutions

AI Indoor Navigation

Guiding visitors to shops, restaurants, restrooms, and services with ease.

Smart Security Integration

Linking cameras and sensors to improve safety and quick emergency response.

Visitor Experience Insights

Data-driven analytics on visitor flow (with privacy safeguards).

Tenant Support

Helping businesses inside malls gain visibility and attract more customers.
