Your privacy matters to us. This Policy explains how InovaRoute collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our Services.

Information We Collect

Account Information

  • name;
  • email;
  • login credentials.

Building & Mapping Data

  • uploaded floor plans;
  • POIs;
  • route graphs;
  • map settings.

Device Information

  • browser;
  • device type;
  • operating system.

Usage Information

  • search activity;
  • navigation interactions;
  • diagnostics.

Location Information

Only collected when permission is granted.

How We Use Information

We use information to:
