About Us

The Company Behind the Intelligence

InovaRoute is a technology-driven company focused on developing AI-powered indoor navigation and smart facility solutions. Our mission is to make malls, airports, hospitals, and other large complexes easier, safer, and smarter for visitors, tenants, and management teams across Africa.

Founded by Victor Kyeva and based at 84 Riverside Drive, Nairobi, we are building the intelligence infrastructure layer that powers smart buildings across the continent "” starting with the facilities where navigation matters most.

Our four-product platform "” InovaMap, InovaNode, InovaHub, and the Admin Dashboard "” works together to give any large building the intelligence it needs to guide people, track assets, and optimise operations in real time.
